Chris Leatham
UX, Graphic, and Print Designer

About Me

I am a UX, Graphic, and Print Designer who enjoys creating and invisioning world class experiences for clients and users.

School

I grew up in Kent, Washington going to Kentwood High School. Starting my junior year of high school, I started attending Green River Community College to earn some college credit while challenging myself by attending classes. I began to focus my studies on design and psychology. After I graduated high school, I began studying at the University of Washington, majoring in Informatics with a focus on Human Computer Interaction. I will be graduating in the June of 2017.

Work

I started teaching myself graphic design tools and practices since I was in 7th grade. I began designing website designs and application designs when I was in 12th grade. Since then, I have created websites for clients, designed applications for clients, and created many posters, videos, brochures, and more. I have been employed by many companies (such as the University of Washington, Fred Hutch, and T-Mobile) to create designs and experiences for customers.

Personal Facts

I am really into movies, tv shows, and comic books. I often spend my time drawing on my computer and practicing my skills in photoshop and staying up to date on design software. I have been passionate about technology and art since I was a little kid and want will continue to pursue my passion.
